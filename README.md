# FNP-Sales-Analysis
## Executive Summary

Ferns and Petals (FNP) – Sales Analysis Dashboard (2023)

This project analyzes sales data from Ferns and Petals (FNP) to uncover insights into revenue performance, customer behavior, product trends, and operational efficiency across multiple gifting occasions in 2023. The interactive dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key KPIs and make data-driven decisions to improve sales strategy and customer satisfaction.

## Key Business Metrics

- Total Orders: 1,000

- Total Revenue: ₹3,520,984

- Average Order-to-Delivery Time: 5.53 days

- Average Customer Spending: ₹3,521 per order

These metrics indicate a healthy order volume with a moderate delivery cycle, highlighting opportunities for operational optimization.

## Revenue & Sales Performance Insights

**Occasion-Based Revenue:**
Revenue varies significantly by occasion.

- Anniversaries, Raksha Bandhan, and Holi generate the highest revenue.

- Diwali and Valentine’s Day show comparatively lower revenue, indicating scope for targeted promotional campaigns.

**Monthly Revenue Trend:**
Sales demonstrate seasonality, with notable peaks during February and August, aligning with Valentine’s Day and Raksha Bandhan.
Lower sales in mid-year months suggest opportunities for off-season marketing initiatives.

## Product Performance Insights

**Top Categories by Sales:**

- Colors and Soft Toys are the highest-selling categories.

- Sweets and Cakes also contribute strongly to overall revenue.

**Top 5 Products by Revenue:**

- Magnam Set, Quila Gift, and Dolores Gift are the leading revenue contributors.

- These products should be prioritized in promotions and inventory planning.

## Customer & Geographic Insights

**Customer Ordering Behavior:**

Peak ordering hours are observed during mid day and evening, suggesting optimal times for targeted digital marketing and notifications.

**Top 10 Cities by Orders:**

Imphal, Kohima, and Dibrugarh lead in order volume.

These cities represent high-value markets for localized offers and faster delivery services.

## Operational Insights

- The average delivery time of 5.53 days remains consistent across most orders.

- Analysis indicates that higher order volumes do not significantly impact delivery time, reflecting stable logistics performance.

- Further optimization could improve customer experience, especially during peak seasons.

## Business Recommendations

- **Strengthen High-Performing Occasions:**
Invest more in marketing and exclusive bundles for Anniversaries, Raksha Bandhan, and Holi.

- **Boost Low-Performing Events:**
Improve promotions and discounts for Diwali and Valentine’s Day to maximize seasonal revenue.

- **Optimize Product Strategy:**
Focus inventory and advertising on top-performing products and categories.

- **Improve Delivery Experience:**
Reducing delivery time during peak months can increase customer satisfaction and repeat purchases.

- **Leverage City-Level Insights:**
Introduce city-specific campaigns in high-order regions to strengthen market presence.

## Conclusion

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of FNP’s sales ecosystem, enabling leadership to track performance, identify growth opportunities, and optimize operational efficiency. By leveraging these insights, FNP can enhance customer engagement, improve delivery performance, and drive sustained revenue growth.
